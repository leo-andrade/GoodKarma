Daycare Guidelines

All dogs are required to meet these guidelines to be considered for daycare

Must be 12 weeks of age or older.

Up-to-date vaccinations.

Owners must submit written proof from their veterinarian of DHPP and Bordetella vaccinations.

Rabies is required for puppies and dogs over 4 months of age.

Bordetella vaccines are required every 6 months.

All dogs must be in good health.

Upon admission, must be free of any condition that could potentially jeopardize other dogs, including fleas or ticks.

Owners will certify that their dog(s) are in good health and have not been ill with a communicable condition in the last 30 days.

Dogs that have been ill with a communicable condition in the last 30 days will require veterinarian certification of health to be admitted or readmitted.

Non-agressive. No food, toy, or other resource guarding behaviors.

Owners will certify their dogs have not harmed or shown any aggressive or threatening behavior towards any person or dog. The safety and health of all the dogs at Good Karma Dog Center is our responsibility and we take it seriously.

Complete up-to-date daycare application on file.

Temperament test

All dogs must undergo a temperament test performed on the first day your dog attends daycare. Not all dogs are suitable candidates for daycare, for a variety of reasons, which is why we require a temperament test.

Good Karma Dog Center reserves the right to dismiss any dog from daycare, grooming, pet sitting, or training if the dog shows aggressive or threatening behavior at any time.

Grooming

Gentle, Low Stress Dog Grooming

Before

One-on-one low stress grooming session personalized to your dog based on his age, breed, personality, fears, or mobility limitations. Our patient, gentle groomer is recommended for skittish, senior, medically fragile, or injured dogs.

Comfort and confidence are the keys to a successful groom. We take the extra time to ensure your dog is comfortable with the process.

Despite efforts toward low-stress, your dog may benefit by splitting the grooming into two or more sessions. Good Karma is committed to do whatever we can to help your dog learn to accept and hopefully enjoy being groomed.

Only one dog groomed at a time.

No dog is crated all day.

The groomer will notify you when your dog’s groom is completed and you are expected to pick up your dog within 20 minutes.

Prices vary according to breed and coat condition. Our expert groomer is happy to provide a competitive estimate at your request.

Purchase a discounted 5 trim pass and get your 6th trim for free!

Grooming available Tuesday through Saturday by appointment.

Pet Sitting and Dog Walking

Relax in the knowledge that while you are away your precious pooch is in the care of a seasoned professional – one that he knows and likes!

Good Karma offers pet sitting for our clients. Your pup will be in the care of one of our exceptional staff members, not crated and left overnight in a facility without onsite supervision.

We will watch your pet in the comfort of his own home or he is welcome to stay in one of ours. Our reliable trustworthy staff will ensure a safe, secure, and low stress experience for your dog while he is under our care.

You and your pet have the advantage of knowing your caregiver. Your dog has established a relationship with his caregiver at daycare, which will help ease your pet’s stress while you are away from home. We will text you and send photos, if you want a daily update during your absence.

Slots fill up fast, so don’t delay and call us today to reserve time for your pet sitting needs.

Good Karma also offers dog walking for our daycare clients on a continual or intermittent basis.
