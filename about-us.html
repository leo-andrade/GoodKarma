About Good Karma Dog Center

Good Karma provides exceptional daycare for your canine companion. She will learn basic manners, too, while cavorting with her dog and human friends.

Your dog is not just another warm body at Good Karma. We get to know your dog, her likes, dislikes, and idiosyncrasies, so that she feels safe and comfortable in our care.

Good Karma provides:

  • A clean, safe, and stimulating environment for your dog.
  • Individualized attention. Consistent supervision. Smaller supervised groups.
  • Anti-fatigue, environmentally-friendly rubber flooring.
  • Temperament test to ensure daycare is the right fit for your dog.
  • Dog Training. Beginner to advanced levels. In-house or one-on-one.
  • Pet Sitting for our clients, either in your home or ours. We do not board dogs at our facility
  • Grooming - One-on-one grooming sessions. Low stress handling techniques. Gentle treatment for senior or medically fragile dogs. No crating.
  • Indoor & outdoor play areas. Securely fenced. Dogs separated by temperament and size
  • Zen room for less active or special needs pets.
  • Bonded and insured.
  • Conveniently located north of the 84th Street Exit off I-80.
